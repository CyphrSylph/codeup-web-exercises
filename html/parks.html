Traversal Location FAQs

Can mounts traverse to every location?
Flying mounts will be temporarily unavailable while in Rads-at-Han.
How many aetherytes are located in Labyrinthos?
There are three aetherytes that can be found in The Archeion, Sharlayan Hamlet, and Aporia
How many aetherytes are located in Thavnair?
There are three aetherytes that can be found in Yedlihmad, The Great Work, and Palaka's Stand
What is the climate like in Radz-at-Han?
The island has sweltering days and muggy evenings.
Are there any locations with active trials?
The Mothercystal is a level 89 trial located in Thaumzein, Labyrinthos.
Where are the best places to visit in Thavnair?
The Hamsa Hatchery, Giantsgall Grounds, Kadjaya's Footsteps, and The Font of Maya are top places of interest.
Which location has the best market?
Radz-at-Han is a prosperous and major trading hub of the Near Eastern Isle.
What is 'The Living Library'?
Sharlayans use Labyrinthos as a storage facility or main repository to contain books, scrolls, live specimens, and forum-based experiments.
